HOW TO INSTALL

Get Roshade up and running in under 2 minutes.

01

Download

Get the ZIP and extract it to any folder.

02

Enable Dev Mode

Open chrome://extensions and turn on Developer Mode.

03

Load folder

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder.

04

Enable Shaders

Pin Roshade, open Roblox, click the icon, and hit Enable Shaders.
